Our Courses

Basic to Advanced Diploma Course

  • This course is designed to teach the fundamentals of makeup artistry and progress to more advanced techniques.
  • Students will learn everything from color theory, facial anatomy, and skin preparation to corrective makeup, high-fashion looks, and special effects.

Proportional Makeup Course

  • This course focuses on the principles of proportion, balance, and symmetry in makeup application.
  • Students will learn how to create looks that flatter different face shapes and features, as well as how to enhance or correct imbalances.

Basic to Advanced Beauty Course

  • This course covers a range of beauty treatments and services, including skincare, hair styling, nail care, and makeup application.
  • Students will learn techniques for basic maintenance, as well as more advanced skills for special occasions and editorial work.
